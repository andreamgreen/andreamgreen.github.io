Impact of Sustainable Retrofitting on Resident Wellbeing: A Critical Review
J.F. Xu, A. Green, S. Jain, D. Chan, S.L. Billington
Building and Environment, 2023
[PDF]
This review synthesizes existing literature on how sustainable building retrofits influence resident health and wellbeing. It reveals significant gaps in post-retrofit evaluation and proposes a framework for holistic impact assessment.
A Human-Centered Perspective on Optimizing Ambient Assisted Living Sensing Systems for Aging in Place
A. Green, A. Cuadra, S.L. Billington, Y. Dong
HumanSys, 2025
[PDF]
This work explores design tradeoffs in sensing systems intended for aging-in-place environments, emphasizing user-centered considerations like privacy, control, and adaptability across diverse populations of older adults.
